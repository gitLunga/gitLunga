## 📌 Featured Projects

### 🏛 DOJCD Connect — *Flagship Project*
A three-application government device procurement platform built for the Department of Justice during my internship at Malcam ICT Solutions, under the MICT SETA programme. Drove the majority of commits across all three repos from first commit through to production deployment.
✅ **Tech:** Node.js, Express, React, React Native, PostgreSQL
✅ **Features:** Role-based access control, SLA monitoring, multi-stage approval workflows, full audit trails

### 🛒 Malcam Commerce Platform *(in progress)*
A multi-tenant SaaS e-commerce platform letting any business onboard with its own isolated storefront, product catalog, staff, and customers. Built as a monorepo with two React/Vite SPAs (storefront + admin dashboard) on a TypeScript/Express API.
✅ **Tech:** TypeScript, Node.js, Express, React, Prisma, PostgreSQL
✅ **Features:** Row-level multi-tenancy, subdomain-based tenant resolution, custom JWT auth, atomic stock deduction, platform admin analytics suite

### 🌾 Farmora — *MICT SETA National Skills Challenge Hackathon Winner*
AgriTech solution built with a 7-member cross-institutional team, earning national recognition at the MICT SETA National Skills Challenge Hackathon.

### 🛠 Technical Support Logging System (Techtrackers)
